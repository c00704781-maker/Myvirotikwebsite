How to Download TikTok Video
in MP4

Use this guide to save public TikTok videos through ViroTik on iPhone, Android, Safari, Chrome, and desktop browsers.

1

Copy the link

Open the public TikTok video, press Share, then choose Copy link.

2

Paste it in ViroTik

Open ViroTik, paste the link in the downloader box, then press Download Now.

3

Choose MP4 quality

Select the available quality and close the ad screen to start the normal download prompt.
