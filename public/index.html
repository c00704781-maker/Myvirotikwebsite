Frequently Asked Questions

No. Private, deleted, region-blocked, or login-only videos cannot be downloaded.

Do I need to sign up?

No sign-up and no payment are required.

Why does a link fail?

The video may be private, deleted, restricted, or temporarily unavailable. Try another public link.

Does ViroTik show ads?

Yes, but the main flow uses one controlled ad screen after quality selection.
