About ViroTik

ViroTik is an online TikTok video downloader built for public TikTok links. It helps users paste a TikTok URL, choose an available MP4 quality, and save the file from their browser.

What ViroTik does

ViroTik focuses on a simple downloader flow: paste link, parse video, choose quality, then download. It is designed for mobile users on Safari and Chrome as well as desktop browsers.

Public videos only

ViroTik does not support private, deleted, restricted, or login-only videos. Users should only download content they have rights or permission to save.
